Session 16

CASE STUDIES ANALYSIS

Goals of this session

  • To analyse local cases using the actual text of the VGGT
  • To identify keywords or entry points from the tenure cases where a link can be made to the text of the VGGT
  • To identify potential uses of the VGGT in relation to the case and areas of action for their potential use.

Timing

2hours 30 minutes.

Explain the purpose of the session.

Distribute the Annex "Fold-out User Guide" of the People’s Manual and explain how to use it.

Split the participants into four or five working groups. Ask each group to select one of the existing case studies from Session 6 to work on.

Ask groups to undertake the case study analysis, by:

  1. Reviewing the case and identifying the mainthemes, actors and policies
  2. identifying keywords related to the main topics of the VGGT
  3. Finding thekeywords/entry points in the "Fold-out User Guide" of the People’s Manual and the relatedchapter/paragraph of the VGGT
  4. Reading the chapters/paragraphs of the VGGT to become familiar withthe document
  5. Identifying areas of action related to the case based on knowledge of the VGGT.

Ask a representative from each group to present the group’s results to the plenary and discuss them.

What you need

Resources

  • Summary of selected cases (handout prepared by the facilitator, as developed in Session 6).
  • The flipcharts or summaries of local cases from Session 6 and the flipcharts showing the mapping of actors from Session 7 and Session 12.
  • "Fold-out User Guide" of the People’s Manual to be distributed to all participants (See http://www.foodsovereignty.org/wp-content/uploads/2016/06/peoplesmanual_annex.pdf).

Equipment

  • Laptop and projector

Tips and comments

  • It is also possible to prepare this session based on Chapter 2 "When conflicts arise, what do the Guidelinestell us?" of the People’s Manual.
