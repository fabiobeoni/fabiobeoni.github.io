THE TRAINING SESSIONS | Preliminaries  

Session 1

WELCOME AND OPENING

Goals of this session

  • To mark the formal opening of the training
  • Tomotivate the participants and inform them about the higher-levelobjectives of the workshop in the national context

Learning objectives

Explain the purpose of the session, then divide the participants into the same working groups as in Session 6 and provide each group with a flipchart.

Timing

30 minutes

The steps

  • Step 1
  • 1
  • Step 2
  • 2

Beforethe workshop, agree on an appropriate speaker(s) and invite him/herto make introductory remarks of approximately 10–15 minutes.

Thefacilitator (usually one of the organizers) introduces thespeaker(s), who makes the opening speech.

What you need

Training resources

Briefing note forthe speaker(s).

Equipment

  • Laptopcomputer (if the speech is based on a PowerPoint presentation)
  • Projector(if the speech is based on a PowerPoint presentation)
  • Microphone

Tips and comments

  • During this session, it could also be useful to clarify the role of any co-organizing institution
  • Consider gender balance if inviting more than one speaker
  • It would be useful if the speaker introduced the importance of implementing the VGGT in the country, to better understand the context and rationale of the workshop
