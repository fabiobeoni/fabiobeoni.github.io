THE TRAINING SESSIONS | Principles of the Voluntary Guidelines

Session 12

USE OF THE VOLUNTARY GUIDELINES

Goals of this session

  • To provide information on the possible use of the VGGT and some examples of such use in various countries around the world
  • To brainstorm about possible uses of the VGGT at country level

Timing

40 minutes.   

The steps

  • Step 1
  • 1
  • Step 2
  • 2
  • 3
  • 4
  • Step 3
  • Step 4

Explain the purpose of the session.

Present some examples of possible uses of the VGGT using the digital presentation provided: VGGT use and status of implementation worldwide.

Divide the participants into four groups and ask them to identify ways the VGGT could be used in their country.

Ask each group to present the results of its discussion and reinforce the main points by writing them on a flipchart. Wrap-up the discussion by summarizing the main ideas.

What you need

Resources

  • Digital presentation: VGGT use and status of implementation worldwide

Equipment

  • Laptop computer and projector
  • Flipchart
  • Markers.

Tips and comments

This session could also be enriched by providing examples of how the VGGT have been used in the country where the training is taking place.

  • Refer to Chapter 3 of the People’s Manual for concrete examples on how theVGGT could be used
  • Further complement and/or update the information provided in the presentation by accessing the VGGT newsletter (see: http://www.fao.org/nr/tenure/whats-new/en/ )
