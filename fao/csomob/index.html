Putting the Voluntary Guidelines on Tenure into Practice

A LEARNING FRAMEWORK FOR CIVIL SOCIETY

ABOUT THIS E-LEARNING GUIDE

Welcome to this e-learning guide for civil society organizations. Here, you will find a methodology and a set of materials that you can use to enhance the competencies of civil society actors, from the grassroots to the national level, to implement the Voluntary Guidelines on the responsible governance of tenure (VGGT).

Through this framework, your trainees will learn how to apply the VGGT to actual tenure governance challenges.
